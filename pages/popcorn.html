Popcorn is a cine-oriented online service that operates via mobile apps. The platform is primarily used to book movie tickets, share reviews and thoughts toward the movies. Popcorn is born to fill in the gap in the movie based online service industry which offers movie ticket booking, online payment and communication.

Challenges:

  • Eliminate the barrier between online ticket booking and social media platform
  • Create a cohesive user interface for both familiar and unfamiliar users
  • Provide a seamless and linear booking experience
  • Provide an engaging and helpful social media platform

Starting the design

User journey

I conducted a basic user flow that would smoothly goes from start to finish. This way helps us to understand how users interact with the product and navigate through the app.

Wireframe

After building the main user flow, I started designing the wireframe. This process is the preparation for the usability testing to review what the pros and cons and what need to be improved.

Usability testing

The usability testing was conducted to figure out what specific difficulties users encounter when they try to complete the core tasks of the Popcorn app: item selection, booking tickets, and in-app navigation.

Refining the design

UI design

Getting started

The "Getting started" was created to be as simple as possible. "Register" and "Sign in" were combined to be familiar with new and returning users. By inputting their phone numbers and email addresses, users would be sent codes to get into the app. If this is the first time, users had to go through a small setting up process. No password needed.

Stories

If the actual cinema locations have posters, standee, or any product placement, Popcorn would like to introduce Stories. Stories were created to promote movies by showing their posters, behind-the-scenes, press interview, etc. This benefits the user experience by adding more promotional content in the app.

Movies

Of course, the most important feature is ticket booking. The booking process was simplified and smoothly touched with the approriate iconography and copy to familiarized the experience. I feel that the app had achieve just that.

Cinemas

Besides, to enhance the experience, I added a booking process starting from the Cinemas. I noticed that there is a group of movie lovers fancy on picking the cinema theaters first then the movies. I want to take care of users right from the start of the booking process.

Critics

Critics is where movie enthusiasts communicate and share their oppinions about movie-related topics. Popcorn offers a constructive and specialized environment for those who love movies and sharing as well as stands out as a one-step app for movie lovers.

You

Lastly, You was designed for account management and supports. This is where users set up their public account, add or edit the account details, control the notification settings, and ask for supports. The iconography, writing and images were carefully selected to be recognizable and understandable so that users would have a delightful experience when setting up their account details. Users can sign out from their account here, too.
