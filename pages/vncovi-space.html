vncovi.space is a dual collaborative project on Covid-19 statistics in Vietnam. vncovi.space aims to create a space for Vietnamese to follow and have a clear view of the Covid-19 pandemic over time.

Understanding the users

We conducted our qualitative research on a group of 7 young Vietnamese people on their worries about the Covid-19 pandemic in Vietnam and the channels they use to update information about the Covid-19 pandemic.

Pain points:

  • All of the interviewees worry about the Covid-19 pandemic in Vietnam but little really know well where to update all the statistics
  • Most of the interviewees need a visual space to update the Covid-19 statistics daily

Impact

The website went live at vncovi.space. Although it did not become well-known, it is used practically by our friends, peers, and families.

What I learned

vncovi.space is the first project I took on the front-end developing responsibility. Became a UX/UI designer from a graphic designer, I have little been exposed to coding. This project made an impact on advancing my front-end coding skills to fulfill my skillset as a UX/UI designer as well as be a chance to practice my design and research skills.

Next steps

The website is scheduled to run for at least a year to provide us and our friends, peers, and families with real-time statistics on the Covid-19 pandemic in Vietnam.
